Brief history – Beach Volleyball

  • 1915 – First beach volleyball games in Hawaii, played with 6 players per side.
  • 1920/30 – Played in Santa Monica. Two-on-two first mentioned (Paul Johnston).
  • 1948 – First official two-man tournament in Santa Monica (crate of Pepsi).
  • 1950/60 – Becomes lifestyle sport in California.
  • 1996 – Olympic sport at Atlanta Games.
  • 2000 – FIVB World Tour → today’s Beach Pro Tour. Prize money main income source.

Main rules – Beach Volleyball

  • Court & Net: 8×16 m, men 2.43 m, women 2.24 m
  • Scoring: Best of 3, sets to 21, tie-break to 15 (win by 2)
  • Players: 2 per team
  • Contact: 3 touches, no double contacts (block exception)
  • Coach: Instructions allowed between rallies
  • Net serves: Live if ball crosses net
