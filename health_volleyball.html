Volleyball – great for kids

YOUTH FRIENDLY
  • No contact sport – lower injury risks compared to football, basketball, boxing and many others.
  • Helps the development of motor skills.
  • Starting at school – life long opportunities, suitable for all sexes, ages and locations.
  • Helps to socialize and interact with others, especially important in times over social media challenges.
PHYSICAL

Physical benefits

  • Strengthen muscles
  • Improve eye - leg - arm coordination
  • Improve reflexes
  • Improve agility and flexibility
  • Helps weight control a.o.
MENTAL

Mental benefits

  • Improve discipline and patience
  • Improve fast decision making
  • Helps to control stress and anxiety
  • Improve self-esteem
  • Improve focus and concentration
SOCIAL

Social benefits

  • Improves team spirit, teamwork and communication
  • Fun way to stay active with friends
  • Builds friendships behind the court, reduction of social isolation
  • Teaching fairness, respect, dedication, mutual support and leadership skills
  • Community building
